Project / Works.

RePlate

A website solution to food waste.

'RePlate' is a social media platform on the web that allows you to connect with people in your area to either donate, or be a recipient of this food. Once in the site, you are able to share posts advertising food items that you have in excess. You can also see all donations posted by other users in a feed scroll view and are able to accept these donations. Built with Vue.js. Project Link.

Pikitup

A website to collect recyclable wastes from your doorstep.

Pikitup is a solution for everyone to start recycling reusable scraps. Starting from our home! Pikitup is a web-based app, anyone can access it at anytime, anywhere, and with any device. Users can order a "driver" to pick and collect their waste at the doorstep. The reusable waste will be exchanged with in-app coins which can be used to buy interesting items available in the web-app. Built with React.js. Project Link.
